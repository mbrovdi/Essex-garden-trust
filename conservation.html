Our Conservation Work

We work to protect and restore the historic gardens and landscapes of Essex, ensuring they can be enjoyed by future generations.

Our conservation efforts include:

  • Surveying and documenting historic gardens and landscapes
  • Providing advice and support to garden owners and managers
  • Advocating for the protection of important garden sites
  • Organizing volunteer conservation projects

By supporting our conservation work, you help us preserve the beauty and heritage of Essex's gardens for everyone to enjoy.
